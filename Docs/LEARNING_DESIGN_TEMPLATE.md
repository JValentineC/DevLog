Adult Learning Design Process
Set Intention:  What do I want learners to be able to do as a result of this learning experience? (learning objectives)
Clear connection to i.c. stars learning objectives
Explicit narration of the learning goals of the session
Start With Why:  Why is this learning critical to their work?
Explicit narration of why the learning goals matter to the learner’s current/future work
Emotional or Experiential Hook:  How will I elicit connection and curiosity to the content?  What do I know about what learners are navigating that I can integrate into myopening?  
Story or Learner Experience 
Key/Core Content:  What key/core content needs to be explicitly taught?
Explain key/core content
Model key/core content
Application:  What application practice will I build into the learning experience to enable skill development?
Guided practice with explicit narration of steps; and/or
Partner practice with feedback; and/or
Independent practice with feedback  
Assessment and/or Debrief:  How will I know that learners accomplished the learning objective?  How will I promote reflection and strengthen learner self-awareness and empathy?
Performance task (and rubric or checklist)
Facilitated dialogue w/ planned questions 
Written reflection 
Retrieval Practice:  What practice/retrieval will I follow up with in one week?
Performance task (and rubric or checklist)
Extension and Support:  What extensions can learners independently explore if they are eager to deepen their learning?  What application support will be provided for those who struggle with retrieval one week following the learning session?
Non prescribed extension opportunities focused on application or key/core content (at least three options- learner chooses)
Support opportunities focused on application (at least two- learner chooses)
